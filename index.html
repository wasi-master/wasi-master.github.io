About

My name is Arian Mollik Wasi, also known on the internet as Wasi Master. I was born on 18 April 2007 in Bangladesh and I'm a hobbyist programmer and photographer. I fell in love with computers ever since I used one, I really like technology. I wrote my first line of code when I was 11 years old and started working on my first large project on April 2020. I am fluent in English and my native language is Bengali, I can also speak a moderate amount of Hindi. Although I have used a huge chunk of programming languages that exist today, I would say I am most proficient in Python and second most would be probably HTML and/or CSS and/or JavaScript. As for photography, my father bought me a Canon PowerShot series camera in 2015 and I didn't really use it professionally then (nor do I now), it was just something that I had and used from time to time to take nature photos. In 2020 I bought a Canon EOS M50 and with the Viltox speed-booster thing, I can use any EF-M, and EF lens on it. I have since been taking photography more seriously and I do use this camera more often and take it to places more. I mostly shoot portraits so I bought a 50mm f/1.8 (which works like a 35mm f/1.2 if talking in APS-C terms) to take my photography to the next level. If you want to contact me for some reason you can send me an email at arianmollik323@gmail.com but keep in mind that I do not check my email every day so you may have to wait a few days for a reply
